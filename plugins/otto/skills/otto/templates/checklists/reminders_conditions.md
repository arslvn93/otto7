# Conditions Reminder Doc

**Template Type:** Document — a printable / copyable list of conditions, waiver dates, and reminders the agent can drop into their calendar
**Tone:** Utility doc, no fluff
**When to use:** Immediately after a conditional offer is accepted

---

## Required Details

- Property address
- Client name(s)
- Side (buyer/seller)
- Firm/conditional offer accepted date
- List of conditions and their waiver deadlines
- Deposit amount + deadline
- Closing date

## Conditions & Waiver Deadlines

### Condition 1 — [e.g., Financing]
- **Waiver deadline:** [Date, 6:00 PM]
- **Responsible:** [Buyer's lender / Buyer's lawyer / Buyer]
- **Action items:**
  - [ ] Confirm lender has accepted agreement
  - [ ] Confirm appraisal booked (if required)
  - [ ] Get written financing confirmation from lender
  - [ ] Review with client
  - [ ] Sign waiver document
- **Calendar reminders:**
  - 72 hours before deadline: Check in with lender
  - 24 hours before deadline: Confirm waiver ready to sign
- **Status:** ☐ Open   ☐ Waived   ☐ Extended to [date]

---

### Condition 2 — [e.g., Home Inspection]
- **Waiver deadline:** [Date, 6:00 PM]
- **Responsible:** Buyer / Agent
- **Action items:**
  - [ ] Book inspection with [Inspector Name]
  - [ ] Attend last 30 min of inspection
  - [ ] Review report with client
  - [ ] Decide: waive, negotiate, or walk
  - [ ] Sign waiver document (or amendment)
- **Calendar reminders:**
  - Day of inspection: confirm time
  - Day after inspection: review report
  - 24 hours before deadline: waiver or negotiation decision
- **Status:** ☐ Open   ☐ Waived   ☐ Renegotiated   ☐ Walked

---

### Condition 3 — [e.g., Status Certificate Review (Condo)]
- **Waiver deadline:** [Date, 6:00 PM]
- **Responsible:** Lawyer review
- **Action items:**
  - [ ] Order status certificate from listing agent
  - [ ] Forward to client's lawyer on receipt
  - [ ] Review lawyer feedback
  - [ ] Discuss red flags with client
  - [ ] Sign waiver document
- **Calendar reminders:**
  - Day status cert is ordered: confirm 10-day clock
  - 72 hours before deadline: confirm lawyer review complete
- **Status:** ☐ Open   ☐ Waived   ☐ Renegotiated   ☐ Walked
