# Buyer Onboarding Survey

**Template Type:** Intake survey — sent to buyer after the initial consultation
**Tone:** Warm, practical, easy to fill out. Think: gathering everything we need to hunt effectively.
**When to use:** Day 1–2 after the buyer consultation, alongside the welcome email.

---

## Required Details

- Buyer name(s)
- Preferred contact method
- Whether the consultation already happened

---

## Survey Template

**Subject line:** A few quick questions so I can find you the right home

---

**Email intro:**

Hi [Buyer Name(s)],

Great chatting with you. Before I start sending you listings, I want to make sure I understand exactly what you're looking for so I'm not wasting your time on homes that aren't a fit. The more detail you give me here, the sharper my search gets.

Reply to this email, text, or fill it out on your phone — whatever's easiest.

---

**1. The basics**
- Ideal move-in / closing date
- Hard deadline (lease ending, job start, etc.)
- Are you buying together? If so, who's the decision maker — both, or one of you?
- How do you prefer to be reached — text, call, email?

**2. Price & financing**
- Max comfortable price
- Ideal price range (where you're happiest spending)
- Pre-approved? With which lender? What's the cap?
- Down payment ready? From where (savings, gift, sale of current home)?
- Any programs you're using (first-time buyer, FHSA, RRSP, etc.)?

**3. Location**
- Top 3 neighborhoods (in order of preference)
- Neighborhoods you'd also consider
- Neighborhoods that are a no for any reason
- Commute considerations — where do you work, how do you get there, max commute you'd accept?
- Walkability / transit / car dependency preferences

**4. The home itself**
- Minimum bedrooms / bathrooms
- Minimum square footage (or open to smaller if layout is great)
- Property type (detached, semi, townhouse, condo, open)
- Parking (how many spots, garage vs driveway)
- Outdoor space (must-have, nice-to-have, don't care)
- Basement (finished, separate entrance, income potential, don't care)
- Age of home (new only, open to character homes, no major reno appetite)

**5. Must-haves and dealbreakers**
- 3 things the home absolutely MUST have
- 3 things that would make you walk away immediately
- Any specific features (home office, mudroom, ensuite, walk-in closet, etc.)

**6. Lifestyle context**
- Any pets we should plan around?
- Do you work from home? Full-time or hybrid?
- Hobbies or interests that affect the home (big chef's kitchen, gym space, workshop, etc.)
- Anything about your situation I should know that makes this search different from a typical one?

**7. Your search so far**
- Have you been actively looking? For how long?
- Any homes you've already seen and liked (or didn't)?
- Are you set up on any listing alerts elsewhere?

---

**Close:**

That's it. Send it back whenever works. The moment I have your answers, I'll set up a saved search and you'll start seeing properties that actually match — no more generic listing spam.

Excited to find you the right one.

[Agent Sign-Off]

[Signature Block]

---

## Tone Notes for Claude

- Never ask about family size, number of children, ages, religion, national origin — Fair Housing no-go
- Keep questions practical and house-related
- Use "we" language to make the search feel collaborative
- Under 700 words total
