# Go-To-Market Prep Email (Seller)

**Template Type:** Email — sent 2–5 days before listing goes live
**Tone:** Clear, operational, reassuring. The seller should know exactly what happens when the switch flips.
**When to use:** After welcome email and pre-listing prep, a few days before launch.

---

## Required Details

- Seller name(s)
- Property address
- Target list date (launch day)
- Showing system (Showing Time, appointment only, etc.)
- Lockbox details (yes/no, location, code handling)
- First open house date if booked
- How feedback will be communicated

---

## Email Template

**Subject line options:**
- Launch day prep — here's how showings will work
- Quick rundown before we go live on [list date]
- Everything you need to know before [Property Address] hits the market

---

**Email body:**

Hi [Seller Name(s)],

We're almost there. [Property Address] goes live on **[Target List Date]**, and I want you to know exactly what to expect so there are no surprises.

**How showings will work**

- All showing requests come through [Showing Time / ShowingPro / direct to me]. You'll get a notification when an appointment is requested.
- Standard notice will be **[X hours]**, unless you tell me a specific window is off-limits.
- You can approve or decline any showing from your phone — or I can handle all of them for you, just tell me your preference.
- [If lockbox] A combo lockbox will be placed on [location] on [date]. The code is only shared with showing agents through the system — it is never texted or emailed.
- [If appointments only] Showings will be by appointment only with me or my team present.

**What I need from you on showing days**

- Home tidy, lights on, blinds open
- Pets crated or out of the home if possible
- Leave the home during showings (buyers talk more freely when sellers aren't there)
- Temperature comfortable — 68–72°F is the sweet spot

**How I'll communicate with you**

- Every showing → I'll send you the feedback as soon as the agent submits it, usually same-day or next morning
- Every week → you'll get a written update from me summarizing showings, feedback patterns, and anything the market is telling us
- Offers → you'll hear from me within minutes, not hours

**[If open house booked]** First open house is **[date/time]**. I'll handle setup, signs, follow-up — you just need to be out of the home for those hours.

**[If not booked]** We're holding off on open houses until we see how the first week goes. I'll let you know once we make a plan.

**A quick reminder about security**

- Lock up any prescriptions, jewelry, cash, and important documents
- Family photos with names or school info are better tucked away
- If you have a smart doorbell or interior camera, let me know — some buyers ask about it

Launch day is **[Target List Date]**. Text me any time with questions between now and then. We've got this.

[Agent Sign-Off]

[Signature Block]

---

## Tone Notes for Claude

- This is the operational handoff — reassuring, specific, not salesy
- Adapt sections based on what the intake provided (drop the open house section if none booked)
- Keep it under 450 words
- Never use "rest assured," "unprecedented," or "navigate the market"
