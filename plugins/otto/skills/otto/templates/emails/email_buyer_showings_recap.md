# Buyer Showings Recap Email

**Template Type:** Email — sent after a batch of showings (3+ homes in one day or week)
**Tone:** Organized, honest, a little coaching. Helps the buyer process what they saw instead of blurring them together.
**When to use:** Same day as the showings, or the following morning at the latest.

---

## Required Details

- Buyer name(s)
- List of homes shown (address + short identifier)
- Buyer's verbal reactions during the tour
- Agent's read of which are serious contenders

---

## Email Template

**Subject line:** Recap — the [#] homes we saw [today / this weekend]

---

**Email body:**

Hi [Buyer Name(s)],

Quick recap while everything's still fresh. We saw [#] homes [today / this weekend]. Here's how I'd score them based on what you said in the car and what I saw on your faces inside.

---

**1. [Address #1]** — Listed at [price]

*What you liked:* [specific thing buyer said]
*What gave you pause:* [specific thing]
*My read:* [Serious contender / worth a second look / probably not]
*Next step if you want to move:* [inspection, offer, pass]

---

**2. [Address #2]** — Listed at [price]

*What you liked:* [specific thing]
*What gave you pause:* [specific thing]
*My read:* [...]
*Next step:* [...]

---

**3. [Address #3]** — Listed at [price]

*What you liked:* [specific thing]
*What gave you pause:* [specific thing]
*My read:* [...]
*Next step:* [...]

---

**Patterns I noticed**

A few things came up across more than one home that are worth paying attention to:

- [Pattern 1 — e.g., "You gravitate toward homes with a real mudroom, even when you said it was a nice-to-have"]
- [Pattern 2 — e.g., "The basement situation is a bigger deal for you than you initially told me"]
- [Pattern 3]

These aren't critiques — they're signals. If I adjust my search based on what I'm actually seeing you respond to, we'll find the right one faster.

**My honest take**

[One paragraph of candid assessment. Examples:
- "Of the three, [Address] is the one I'd keep an eye on. Want to drive by again or schedule a second showing?"
- "Honestly, none of these three are it. I've already got three more to show you next week that I think are closer."
- "You told me you loved [Address], but your body language inside said otherwise. Want to talk about that?"]

**Homework for you**

- Look at the photos again tonight — sometimes a home grows or shrinks in your mind after you've seen it
- Tell me which one, if any, you'd want to revisit
- If any of them are a hard no, say so — it helps me calibrate

Reply whenever you're ready. No rush unless one of them is a serious contender and the market is moving fast — in which case, call me.

[Agent Sign-Off]

[Signature Block]

---

## Tone Notes for Claude

- The "patterns I noticed" section is the differentiator — it shows the agent was paying attention, not just opening doors
- Be honest in "my read" — don't rank every home as a contender
- Keep it under 500 words
- Adapt the number of properties to what the agent provides
