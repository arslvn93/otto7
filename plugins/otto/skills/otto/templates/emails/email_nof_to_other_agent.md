# Notice of Fulfilment — Email to Other Agent

**Template Type:** Email — agent-to-agent, delivering the signed NOF to the other side
**Tone:** Professional, brief, no fluff. This is a paperwork delivery.
**When to use:** Immediately after the client returns the signed NOF.

---

## Required Details

- Other agent name + brokerage
- Property address
- Client name(s)
- Closing date
- Attached signed NOF document

---

## Email Template

**Subject line:** Signed NOF attached — [Property Address] (now FIRM)

---

**Email body:**

Hi [Other Agent First Name],

Please find attached the signed **Notice of Fulfilment** for **[Property Address]**. All conditions have been satisfied and waived.

**Deal summary:**
- Property: [Property Address]
- [Buyer / Seller]: [Client Name(s)]
- Closing: [Closing Date]
- Status as of delivery of this email: **FIRM**

Please confirm receipt when you get a moment, and forward to your [seller / buyer] and their lawyer.

If there's anything else you need from my end to keep things moving toward closing, let me know.

Thanks,

[Agent Sign-Off]

[Signature Block]

---

## Tone Notes for Claude

- Strictly professional, agent-to-agent
- Keep it short — under 150 words
- No celebration language; this is paperwork delivery
- Always ask for receipt confirmation
