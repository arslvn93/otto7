# Purchaser Visit Request Email

**Template Type:** Email — request from buyer's side to listing side for a pre-closing visit
**Tone:** Polite, specific, low-friction. Goal is to make it easy for the listing agent to say yes.
**When to use:** 5–10 days before closing, once the deal is firm.

---

## Required Details

- Property address
- Seller's agent name + brokerage
- Buyer name(s)
- Requested date + 2-3 alternate time windows
- Reason for visit (measurements, bringing contractor, walkthrough, etc.)

---

## Email Template

**Subject line options:**
- Purchaser visit request — [Property Address]
- Pre-closing visit request for [Property Address]
- Purchaser access request — [Property Address] (closing [date])

---

**Email body:**

Hi [Listing Agent First Name],

I hope you're well. My buyers, **[Buyer Name(s)]**, are closing on [Property Address] on **[Closing Date]** and would like to request a purchaser visit before closing.

**What they'd like to do:**
[One or two sentences — e.g., "Take some measurements for furniture and window treatments" / "Bring their contractor through for some scoping" / "Walk through the home one more time with their parents who are helping them move."]

**Requested access:**
- **First choice:** [date], [time window — e.g., Saturday March 14 between 10am and noon]
- **Second choice:** [date], [time window]
- **Third choice:** [date], [time window]

They're flexible on exact time if none of those work. Visit should last about [30–60 minutes].

A few things to confirm:

- They'll be accompanied by me (and [contractor/family member] if applicable)
- No one will bring anything into the home or move anything
- If the sellers would prefer to be present, that's totally fine — just let me know
- If there's a preferred way to handle access (lockbox, key pickup, seller present), just tell me what works on your end

Please let me know which window works, and I'll confirm with my clients right away. Really appreciate it — I know this is one more thing at a busy time of the transaction.

Thanks,

[Agent Sign-Off]

[Signature Block]

---

## Tone Notes for Claude

- Professional agent-to-agent tone, NOT client-facing
- Make it easy to say yes: offer specific windows, keep the visit short, be flexible
- Never demand — this is a courtesy request, not a right (unless the agreement specifies pre-closing visits)
- Under 350 words
