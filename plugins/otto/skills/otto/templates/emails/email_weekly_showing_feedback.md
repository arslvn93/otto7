# Weekly Showing Feedback Email

**Template Type:** Email — recurring weekly update to seller while the listing is active
**Tone:** Direct, data-grounded, honest. No sugar-coating if activity is slow — sellers can smell spin.
**When to use:** Same day every week (agent picks — Friday afternoons are common). Starts the week the listing goes live.

---

## Required Details

- Seller name
- Property address
- This week's showing count
- Total showings to date
- Summary of feedback themes (positives + concerns)
- Any offers or serious interest
- Comparable listings that hit the market or sold this week
- Recommendation (hold, adjust, etc.)

---

## Email Template

**Subject line:** [Property Address] — Week [#] update

---

**Email body:**

Hi [Seller Name],

Here's your weekly update on [Property Address].

**This week's activity**

- Showings this week: **[#]**
- Showings to date: **[# total]**
- Open house traffic (if applicable): **[# groups]**
- Online views (if tracking): **[# new]**

**What buyers are saying**

*Positives:*
- [Theme 1]
- [Theme 2]
- [Theme 3]

*Concerns / hesitations:*
- [Theme 1]
- [Theme 2]

I pay more attention to patterns than to one-off comments. When the same thing comes up from three different buyers, that's signal. One buyer not loving something is noise.

**The market this week**

- [New comparable listings that came on the market, with price and condition notes]
- [Comparable listings that sold or had price changes]
- [Anything notable happening in the broader market]

**Where we stand**

[One of the following paragraphs, adapted:]

- *If activity is strong:* We're getting the traffic we expected. Feedback is trending in the right direction. I'd like to hold the course for another week and see if we get an offer.
- *If activity is moderate:* Traffic is decent but we're not closing the loop into offers yet. The feedback is telling us [specific thing]. I'd like to discuss [next move — staging tweak, photo refresh, light price adjustment] when you have 10 minutes.
- *If activity is slow:* I want to be straight with you — we're not seeing the level of interest I expected. Based on the feedback and the new inventory, I think we should have a candid conversation this week about [price / presentation / strategy]. Let me know when you're free for a quick call.

**What I'm doing next week**

- [Specific action 1]
- [Specific action 2]
- [Specific action 3]

Questions any time. I'd rather you ask than wonder.

[Agent Sign-Off]

[Signature Block]

---

## Tone Notes for Claude

- Numbers first, opinions second — sellers trust updates that lead with data
- Never spin low activity as "quality over quantity." Be honest.
- Always end with a clear next step, not just "let me know if you have questions"
- Keep each weekly update under 400 words
- This is a TEMPLATE — the agent will copy and fill in each week. Make the placeholders obvious.
